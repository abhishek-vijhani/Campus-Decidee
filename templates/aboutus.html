About Us


Campus Decidee was established in 1947 in West Pakistan by Late Mr. Mangal Sain which was later on continued by his son Mr. Munshi Ram Sethi. After partition, Mr. Sethi shifted his business to India and continued its ancestral profession by setting a small consulatation unit at Sadar Bazar, Delhi. Slowly and gradually, it gained acceptability and popularity. Encouraged by ever-increasing response from students at large, it has established two Processing Zones in Delhi & Haryana. Campus Decidee is now being recognized as a leading Counsellor of 'Graduate adn Undergraduate Admissions'. It has honestly maintained a track record of serving self-developed quality through its Exclusive Consultancy offices and Online Web Service with the aim of providing high quality services and education to its students. Campus Decidee has strived to set new standards of quality in all of its operations.
