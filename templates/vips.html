Vivekanand Institute of Professional Study

Man Making, Character Building, Nation Building" through meaningful education : the goal as set out by Swami Vivekananda during his life time.
It was in the year 1997, that Dr S C Vats, founder of Vivekananda Institute of Professional Studies (VIPS), met Swami Jitatmananda ji, an eminent educationist , management expert , author of books on “Modern Physics & Vedanta”, and “Indian ethos in Management”, an active social worker and the editor of “Prabudh Bharat” (Awakened India), a journal started by Swami Vivekakanda. It offers courses towards Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech), Master of Technology (M.Tech), Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) and Master of Business Administration (M.B.A) and contains fourteen academic departments with a strong emphasis on scientific and technological education and research.
